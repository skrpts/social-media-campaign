## Capability

Generates multiple headline options for any piece of content, balancing click-worthiness with accuracy. Applies proven headline formulas whilst avoiding clickbait patterns that erode trust.

## When to Use

- Titling blog posts, articles, or newsletter features
- A/B testing headlines for engagement optimisation
- Rewriting underperforming headlines on existing content

## Inputs

Content summary or draft, target audience, platform (blog, email, social), tone guidance

## Outputs

5-10 headline options with rationale for each, categorised by approach (direct, curiosity, benefit-led, question-based)

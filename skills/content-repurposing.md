## Capability

Transforms a single piece of content into multiple formats: blog post to social threads, video script to article, podcast notes to newsletter.

## When to Use

- Maximising ROI on long-form content
- Feeding multiple channels from a single source
- Adapting content for platform-specific audiences

## Inputs

Source content + target format(s) + platform constraints (character limits, image requirements)

## Outputs

Reformatted content per target platform, preserving key messages and adapting tone

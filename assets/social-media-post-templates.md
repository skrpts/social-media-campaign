## Purpose

Reusable templates for common social media post types. Fill in the bracketed sections with your content.

## LinkedIn Templates

### Thought Leadership

```
[Bold opening statement or surprising statistic]

Here's what most people get wrong about [topic]:

[Point 1]
[Point 2]
[Point 3]

The real insight? [Key takeaway in one sentence]

I've been [relevant experience] for [time period], and this pattern shows up consistently.

What's your experience with [topic]? Drop your thoughts below.

#[hashtag1] #[hashtag2] #[hashtag3]
```

### Case Study

```
[Client/project] needed to [solve problem].

The challenge: [describe the obstacle]
The approach: [what you did]
The result: [measurable outcome]

Key lessons:
1. [Lesson 1]
2. [Lesson 2]
3. [Lesson 3]

Full breakdown in the comments.

#[hashtag1] #[hashtag2] #[hashtag3]
```

## Twitter/X Templates

### Insight Thread

```
Tweet 1: [Bold claim or question] — a thread

Tweet 2: [Context and background]

Tweet 3: [Evidence/example 1]

Tweet 4: [Evidence/example 2]

Tweet 5: [Conclusion and CTA]
```

### Quick Tip

```
[Emoji] [Topic] tip:

[One actionable piece of advice in 1-2 sentences]

[Optional: link or reference]
```

## Instagram Templates

### Carousel Post

```
Slide 1 (Cover): [Attention-grabbing title]
Slide 2: [Problem statement]
Slide 3-6: [Solution steps, one per slide]
Slide 7: [Summary/key takeaway]
Slide 8: [CTA — save, share, follow]

Caption:
[Hook — first line that appears before "more"]

[Expand on the value of the carousel content]

[CTA — what should they do next?]

.
.
.
[Hashtag block — 15-25 relevant hashtags]
```

### Single Image Post

```
[Opening hook — max 125 characters for preview]

[2-3 sentences expanding on the image content]

[Personal insight or story]

[CTA]

[Hashtag block]
```
